Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 1, 2025

Welcome to Catharsis Cards. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile app and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Email address, username, and optional avatar.
  • Usage Data: Swipe actions, liked questions, selected categories, and analytics.
  • Device Data: Device model, OS version, and app version.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To register and manage your account.
  • To personalize your experience and save progress.
  • To send notifications (e.g., cooldown reminders).
  • To analyze usage and improve the app.
  • To comply with legal and security obligations.

3. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Until deletion of your account or as required by law.
  • Usage Data: Anonymized and stored up to 24 months.
  • Device Data: Stored up to 12 months for diagnostics.

4. Your Rights

You may access, rectify, delete, or export your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@catharsiscards.app.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

  • Firebase / Google Cloud: Secure data storage.
  • Analytics Providers: Aggregated and anonymized usage statistics.
  • Service Providers: Crash reporting, hosting, and support tools.

6. International Transfers

We use appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) when transferring data internationally.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard encryption and controls, but no method is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy; continued use after changes indicates acceptance.

10. Contact Us

Questions or requests? Email us at findyourcatharsis@gmail.com.
