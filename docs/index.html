Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 1, 2025

Welcome to Catharsis Cards. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile app and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Email address, username, and optional avatar you provide when registering.
  • Usage Data: Swipe actions, liked questions, categories you select, and analytics to improve the app.
  • Device Data: Device model, operating system version, and app version.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To personalize your experience and save your progress.
  • To send you notifications, including cooldown reminders.
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve features.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We never sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Firebase / Google Cloud: To store your profile and app data securely.
  • Analytics Providers: Aggregated & anonymized usage statistics.

4. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and access controls to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

  • You can delete or change your avatar and username in the app’s settings.
  • You may disable notifications in your device settings.
  • Contact us (below) to request data deletion.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy—if so, we’ll post the revised date here. Continued use after changes means you accept them.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or requests regarding this policy, please email us at privacy@catharsiscards.app.
