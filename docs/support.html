FAQs

What is Catharsis Cards?
A guided journaling and reflection app that uses card prompts to help you process thoughts and feelings.

Which devices are supported?
iPhone and iPad running iOS/iPadOS 15.0 or later. An internet connection is required for some features.

How do I restore a purchase or subscription?
On your device go to Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions and ensure your Catharsis Cards subscription is active. In the app, tap Restore Purchases (if shown) or sign in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

Subscriptions & Billing

  • Subscriptions are managed by Apple. You can cancel or change plans in Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions.
  • Free download with optional in-app subscriptions for unlimited usage.
  • Charges are billed to your Apple ID. Refunds are handled by Apple’s Report a Problem portal.

Troubleshooting

  • Force quit the app and reopen.
  • Check your internet connection and that iOS is up to date.
  • Open the App Store and update Catharsis Cards to the latest version.
  • Restart your device.
  • If issues persist, delete and reinstall the app (your active subscription will restore via Apple ID).
  • For sign-in, purchases, or crashes, email us with device model, iOS version, and steps to reproduce.
